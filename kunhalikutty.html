P. K. Kunhalikutty

Minister for Industries & Commerce

P. K. Kunhalikutty is an Indian politician from Kerala and a senior leader of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML). He has been one of the most prominent political leaders in Kerala and has served in various ministerial positions over several decades.

He has represented constituencies in Malappuram district and has played an important role in the development of industry, commerce and infrastructure within the state. His political career spans legislative, administrative and organizational responsibilities.

Kunhalikutty is known for his contributions to economic development, entrepreneurship and investment promotion. He has been associated with several initiatives aimed at strengthening Kerala's industrial growth and employment opportunities.
