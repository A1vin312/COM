K. M. Shaji

Minister for Local Self Government and Rural Development

K. M. Shaji is an Indian politician from Kerala and a senior leader associated with the Indian Union Muslim League. He has been active in public life and has represented constituencies in the Kerala Legislative Assembly.

Over the years, he has participated in legislative affairs, local development initiatives and public welfare programmes. He is known for his involvement in local governance and community development activities.

As a Minister in the Government of Kerala, he oversees departments responsible for local administration, municipal governance and rural development programmes across the state.

Portfolio
