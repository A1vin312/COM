V. E. Abdul Gafoor

Minister for Fisheries, Harbour Engineering and Social Justice

V. E. Abdul Gafoor is an Indian politician from Kerala and a member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He has been actively involved in public service and social development initiatives across the state.

Throughout his political career, he has worked on issues related to coastal communities, welfare programmes and infrastructure development. He is known for his engagement with local communities and public welfare activities.

As Minister, he oversees departments responsible for fisheries development, harbour engineering projects and social justice initiatives aimed at improving the welfare of various sections of society.
