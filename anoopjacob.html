Anoop Jacob

Minister for Food, Civil Supplies, Consumer Affairs and Legal Metrology

Anoop Jacob is an Indian politician from Kerala and the leader of the Kerala Congress (Jacob). He represents the Piravom constituency in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and has been elected multiple times from the constituency. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

He is the son of late T. M. Jacob, a former Kerala minister and founder of Kerala Congress (Jacob). Anoop Jacob entered the Assembly through the Piravom by-election in 2012 and later won consecutive elections from the same constituency. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

In the 2026 Kerala Cabinet led by V. D. Satheesan, he serves as the Minister for Food, Civil Supplies, Consumer Affairs and Legal Metrology. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
