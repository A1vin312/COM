N. Samsudheen

Minister for General Education

N. Samsudheen is an Indian politician from Kerala and a member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He has been actively involved in public affairs, education initiatives and community development.

Throughout his political career, he has worked on matters related to education, literacy and welfare programmes. He has participated in legislative discussions and policy initiatives aimed at improving educational opportunities and social development.

As a Minister in the Government of Kerala, he oversees key departments responsible for school education, literacy programmes, minority welfare and matters related to Waqf and Hajj administration.
