Ramesh Chennithala

Senior Cabinet Minister

Ramesh Chennithala is an Indian politician from Kerala and a senior leader of the Indian National Congress. He has served in various leadership positions in both state and national politics and has been a member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly for several terms.

He began his political journey through student politics and later became one of the prominent political figures in Kerala. Throughout his career, he has held key responsibilities in government and party organizations.

Chennithala is known for his involvement in public administration, governance reforms, social welfare initiatives and legislative affairs. He has played an important role in shaping policies related to development and public service.
