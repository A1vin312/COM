V. D. Satheesan

Chief Minister of Kerala

V. D. Satheesan is an Indian politician and senior leader of the Indian National Congress from Kerala. He has been an active member of the Kerala Legislative Assembly for several terms and is known for his role in state politics and public administration.

He began his political career through student politics and later emerged as a prominent leader in Kerala. Over the years, he has represented the Paravur constituency and has been involved in legislative debates, public welfare initiatives and governance reforms.

As Chief Minister, he heads the Council of Ministers and oversees the administration of the state. His office is responsible for coordinating government departments and implementing policies aimed at economic development, social welfare and infrastructure growth.
