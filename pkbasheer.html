P. K. Basheer

Minister for Public Works Department

P. K. Basheer is an Indian politician from Kerala and a prominent leader of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML). He has represented constituencies in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and has been actively involved in public service and state politics.

Throughout his political career, he has focused on infrastructure development, public welfare and regional development initiatives. He has also played an active role in legislative discussions and policy implementation within the state.

As Minister for Public Works, he is responsible for overseeing roads, bridges, government buildings and major infrastructure projects across Kerala.

Portfolio
  • Public Works Department (PWD)
  • Road Infrastructure
  • Government Buildings
  • Bridge Development
  • State Infrastructure Projects
